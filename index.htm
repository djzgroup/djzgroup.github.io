About Me

I received the Ph.D. degree from the department of computer school, wuhan university, China, in 2015. I am currently a lecturer with the Faculty of Information Engineering, China University of Geosciences, China. Since 2017, I have been serving as a senior member of the China Society for Industrial and Applied Mathematics (CSIAM) and a committee member of the geometric design & computing of CSIAM. My research areas include computer graphics, computer-aided design, computer vision, image and video processing. I have conducted a considerable amount of research in digital geometric processing, computational photography, and he has published more than 20 papers in journals and conferences.
