About Me
Dejun Zhang is currently an associate professor of the College of Computer Science, China University of Geosciences, China. He obtained his Ph.D. degree in Computer Applications Technology under the supervision of Prof. Fazhi He in 2015 from the Department of Computer School at Wuhan University, China. Since 2017, He has been serving as a senior member of the China Society for Industrial and Applied Mathematics (CSIAM) and a committee member of the Geometric Design & Computing of CSIAM. Since 2020, He has been serving as a senior member of the China Computer Federation (CCF). Since 2021, He has been serving as a committee member of the Technical Committee on CAD and Graphics of CCF. His research areas include computer graphics, computer-aided design, computer vision, image and video processing. He has conducted a considerable amount of research in digital geometric processing, computational photography, and he has published more than 50 papers in journals and conferences.
